Top 100 Science Fiction and Fantasy Books According to NPR

Sample application to demonstrate Google's BigQuery and the new JOIN capability in SQL-like queries.

The selection of books and ranking come from npr books and the genres come from Freebase books, associated using Google Refine and its link to Freebase.

There is an explicit AND between the fields. Rank may be entered as an integer or range (e.g. <10)

You can see the full list by just clicking on "Search"
